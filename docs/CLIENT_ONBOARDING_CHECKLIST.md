# Client Onboarding Checklist

Collect this information before starting a new client store.

## Business Information

- Business name
- Tagline or short description
- Phone number
- WhatsApp number
- Support phone or WhatsApp number, if different from sales
- Email address
- Store address
- Facebook page
- Instagram profile
- Other social links

## Brand Assets

- Logo file
- Hero/banner image
- Brand colors
- Preferred fonts, if any
- Product photography style
- Existing brand guidelines, if any

## Domain And Accounts

- Domain registrar access or DNS access
- Vercel account ownership decision
- Supabase account ownership decision
- Resend account ownership decision, if email is included
- Admin email address for the owner

## Products

- Product list
- Product names in English and Bangla, if needed
- Prices and old prices
- Categories
- Product category names the client wants in the shop
- Short descriptions
- Long descriptions
- Product images
- Stock quantities
- Variant options such as size, color, pack, weight, or flavor
- SKU/product codes, if available
- Featured products

## Delivery And Payment

- Delivery zones
- Delivery charges
- Free delivery rules, if any
- COD availability
- bKash account type: merchant or personal
- bKash number and payment instructions
- Nagad account type: merchant or personal
- Nagad number and payment instructions
- Manual payment verification process
- Return/cancellation policy

## Email Decision

- Does the client want email notifications at launch?
- Sender domain available?
- DNS access available for Resend verification?
- Admin notification inbox confirmed?
- Test email recipients confirmed?

## Launch Checklist

- Domain connected
- Supabase migrations applied
- Storage bucket verified
- Admin user created
- `ADMIN_EMAILS` configured
- Products added or imported
- Demo products archived
- Delivery fees verified
- Payment methods verified
- Test order completed
- Order tracking tested
- Admin status update tested
- Product image upload tested
- Mobile QA completed
- Email delivery tested if enabled

## Post-Launch Support

- Admin training completed
- Owner knows how to add/edit/archive products
- Owner knows how to verify payments
- Owner knows how to update order statuses
- Owner knows how customers track orders
- Backup/support contact agreed
- Future feature wishlist recorded
