# Demo Video Outline

Use this guide to record short marketing demos or longer walkthroughs.

## 60-Second Short Demo

1. Show home page and say this is a reusable e-commerce starter pack.
2. Open shop and product detail.
3. Select a variant and add to cart.
4. Open checkout and mention COD, bKash, Nagad.
5. Show order success and tracking page.
6. Jump to admin dashboard.
7. Show orders and payment verification.
8. Show product management and image upload.
9. End with a call to action.

## 3-Minute Full Demo

1. Storefront overview.
2. Shop browsing, search, and product detail.
3. Variant selection and cart.
4. Checkout with manual payment options.
5. Order success and customer tracking.
6. Admin login note, without showing credentials.
7. Admin dashboard.
8. Orders list and order detail.
9. Status update and payment verification.
10. Product list.
11. Product create/edit form.
12. Image upload, inventory, and variants.
13. Explain deployment, Supabase, and optional email setup.
14. End with the starter-pack value proposition.

## Screen Recording Sequence

- Start from a clean browser window.
- Use demo products and demo orders only.
- Keep zoom at 100%.
- Record desktop first.
- Record mobile separately through responsive preview or browser viewport.
- Avoid showing `.env.local`, Supabase dashboard keys, Resend keys, or admin passwords.

## Voiceover Points

- "This is a ready e-commerce starter pack for small businesses."
- "The storefront can be rebranded for baby products, fashion, cosmetics, seasonal products, food items, and retail."
- "Customers can browse, add to cart, check out, and track orders."
- "Store owners can manage products, images, stock, variants, orders, and manual payments."
- "Email notifications are implemented, but live delivery needs the client's verified domain."
- "This can be deployed as a clean demo or cloned for a new client project."

## What Not To Show Publicly

- Admin passwords
- Private admin emails
- Supabase service-role key
- Supabase anon key if shown in env files
- Resend API key
- `.env.local`
- Real customer phone numbers, addresses, or emails
- Real payment account details unless the client approves

## Recommended Final CTA

"If you sell products through Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, or a small shop, this starter pack can give you a professional online store with product management, order management, and customer tracking without starting from zero."
